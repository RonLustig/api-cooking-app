# api-cooking-app
This is a Django-based Cooking App that helps users discover and cook recipes.  The app uses APIs to provide 301 recipes. You can search for recipes by including ingredients you want or excluding ingredients you donâ€™t want. You can also view recipe details on the recipe page. 
# Features
- Browse 301 recipes with cooking instructions and macronutrient information.
- Add or remove recipes from your favorites list.
- Leave reviews on recipes or read reviews from others.
- Create your own recipes to share.
- Search recipes by including or excluding specific ingredients
